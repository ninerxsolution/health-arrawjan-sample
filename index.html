Your health is your most important asset. Protect yourself and your family from medical and surgical expenses with KBZMS Arrawjan Health Insurance.

Health
 
Insurance

Myanmar citizens and citizens of Myanmar who are legally residing in Myanmar. For those between the ages of (6) to (18) years old, including foreigners, by keeping the parents responsible and For those aged (18) to (75) years old, either on their own or on their own. Health insurance can be purchased.

Types of protection and benefits

  • Hospitalization due to illness and hospitalization due to accident. (Hospital cost per day - 10,000 Kyats for 1 unit) (Entitled for up to 60 days)
  • Death due to accident/disease (Kyat 1,000,000 for 1 unit)

Optional insurance coverage

Protection (1)
  • Surgical treatment 1 unit 500,000 kyats
  • Miscarriage 300,000 kyats (one time benefit) (The actual cost is from a minimum of 500,000 Kyats to a maximum amount depending on the unit purchased)
Protection (2)
  • hospital Clinic visits (2500 Kyats for 1 unit/maximum 4 times) are eligible for up to 10,000 Kyats.  
  • Critical illness treatment insurance can be arranged for age groups from (6) to (60) years old. From (6) to (18) years of age can be accommodated by a parent/guardian. (Maximum 10 units) 

Quantity available for purchase

Basic insurance protection

The basic unit can be purchased up to 25 units.

Optional insurance coverage
  • You can purchase up to 25 units of protection (1). 
  • Protection (2) can buy up to 25 units.
